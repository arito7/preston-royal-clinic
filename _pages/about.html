Bunzo Takamatsu, D.O.M., L.AC.
Born in Osaka, Japan, in 1956.
A graduate of Kototama Institute in Santa Fe, New Mexico, where he learned Oriental Medicine and Aikido. A graduate of University of Texas at Dallas with a science degree. A calligrapher, and a Black belt in Aikido and karate. Long time practitioner of Macrobiotic living. Has been studying all kinds of natural healing methods through over 30 years of practice. Raising six children who were all born in Texas.
